Course Summary

Embark on an exciting journey into the world of robotics with our Lego Robotics course featuring Lego WeDo 2.0. Explore the basics of robotics and programming while building your own robots. Dive into creative projects that make learning fun and engaging. Join us to discover the fascinating world of robotics through hands-on exploration with Lego WeDo 2.0.

Requirements

To enroll in our Lego Robotics class featuring Lego WeDo 2.0, you typically need to be between 7 and 12 years old. No prior experience is required, making it suitable for beginners. You'll also need access to a laptop, tablet, or computer to run the Lego WeDo 2.0 software, as well as a Lego WeDo 2.0 kit for building the robots. Most importantly, bring your curiosity and creativity to enjoy learning through hands-on play with robotics.
